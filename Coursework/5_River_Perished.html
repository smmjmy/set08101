You Perished

You kick out with your other leg and hit something solid beneath the water, but the grip around your ankle is solid as iron. Nails dig in hard enough to break skin. It yanks you back, across the stones, and your head once again plunges beneath the water before you can take a breath. The sunlight filtering through the water illuminates the pale face only inches from your own, dirty reeds trailing from its scalp in a mockery of hair. You open your mouth to scream, but the water has already filled your lungs. You are dragged deeper into the murky depths until even the sunlight cannot reach you.
