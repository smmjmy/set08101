Through The Trees

Dare to take a stroll?

Through the Trees is a survivalist/horror choose your own adventure-style quiz.
Your goal is simple: Find a way out of the forest without falling victim to the elements.
Beware, there is more living in these trees than just the birds.

In this game, you choose your own path! Every decision you make alters the route you take and will ultimately decide whether you survive or perish in the trees. There are accompanying ambient tracks on several pages to add to the atmosphere, but these can be toggled on or off depending on your preferences (you may have to allow autoplay in your browser settings). With danger lurking around every corner and 13 possible endings to find, there's only one thing left to say.

Good Luck.
